About me

I am a geophysicist specialized in seismology. My work focuses on active plate boundaries, volcanoes, the cryosphere and geothermal areas.

I am a co-founder at Santa Cruz Geophysics, Inc. and also I am currently working as a postdoc scholar at Colorado State University.

I obtained my bachelors degree in geophysics engineering from the Faculty of Engineering of the National Autonomous University of Mexico in 2014 and my PhD degree from the Earth and Planetary Sciences Department of the University of California, Santa Cruz in 2021.
